# Customer Churn Analysis for Telecom Industry

## Project Overview

Customer churn is a major challenge in the telecom industry, directly impacting revenue and customer growth. This project focuses on analyzing customer behavior, identifying churn patterns, and building a machine learning model to predict customers who are likely to leave the service.

By combining Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA), Data Preprocessing, Machine Learning, and Customer Segmentation, this project provides actionable insights that can help telecom companies improve customer retention and reduce churn rates.

---

## Project Objectives

* Analyze customer demographics and service usage patterns.
* Identify factors influencing customer churn.
* Build a machine learning model to predict customer churn.
* Segment customers based on churn risk.
* Generate business recommendations for customer retention.

---

---

## Key Insights

* Customers with month-to-month contracts exhibit the highest churn rates.
* Customers with shorter tenure are more likely to leave the company.
* Higher monthly charges are associated with increased churn probability.
* Long-term contracts significantly improve customer retention.
* Contract type, tenure, and monthly charges are among the most influential churn factors.

---

## Customer Segmentation

### Loyal Customers

* Long tenure
* Stable service usage
* Low churn probability

### At Risk Customers

* Higher probability of churn
* Short tenure
* Require proactive retention efforts

### Dormant Customers

* Low engagement
* Reduced activity
* Potential candidates for reactivation campaigns

---

## Business Recommendations

* Encourage migration to long-term contract plans.
* Offer loyalty rewards and retention incentives.
* Monitor high-value customers with elevated monthly charges.
* Improve customer support responsiveness.
* Use predictive analytics to identify churn risk early.
